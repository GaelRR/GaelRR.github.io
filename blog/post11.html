I Am Not a Designer, Developer, or Writer—But I Made This Anyway

03.02.2025


I'm not a designer, but I designed this website (and a few other things).

I'm not a developer, but I built this website (and some other projects).

I'm not a writer, but I've written some blog posts and papers.


If you're a designer, you might think this website looks rough. You're probably right.

If you're a developer, you might notice this is just basic HTML and CSS. That's fair.

If you're a writer, you might find my writing a bit messy. I won't argue.


So Why Did I Do It?

I don't set out to create bad work—I promise. I just have other things in life that matter more.

  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family and friends that I love spending time with.
  • 💼 A job that has nothing to do with designing, coding, or writing.
  • 🎸 Hobbies that take me far away from my laptop.

Making Time to Create

My side projects happen in the tiny gaps between everything else. Sometimes I wish I had more time to improve my writing, coding, and design skills—but at the end of the day, it's all about priorities.

For now, my goal is simple: to create with the limited time I have, even if it's imperfect.


So yeah, my work isn't perfect. But at least it exists. And for now, that's enough.

Hope you enjoy my shitty (but improving) work.
