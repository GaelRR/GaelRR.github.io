One of my goals this year was to create and share my personal website. My only requirements:

- Free or as cheap as possible 💰 (I only paid for my domain name)
- Code selv in html, css and JS 💻
- 4 sections: Main page, about, blog & projects
- SoMe links 🔗
- Good enough user experience on both mobile and desktop 📱

The most important was to launch something, iterate on it and have a blog where I could share my process. This website is the result. All the requirements are met. Now it is time to share on the blog and keep iterating as my needs change (Already thinking that a CMS will be a good idea but I don't want to get ahead of myself).

As for now, this website is good enough. Good enough for me.
